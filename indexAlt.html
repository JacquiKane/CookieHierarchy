Cookie Inheritance Workshop

See how to reuse properties and methods. We have a plain cookie, a chocolate chip cookie and a nut chocolate chip cookie. The plain cookie has all the common ingredients, so it makes sense for ChocolateChipCookie class to inherit from a PlainCookie class. NutChocolateChipCookie would inherit from ChocolateChipCookie class. A 3 layer hierarchy!

Click the buttons to see the ingredients you need for the cookie ...

Plain Cookie

2 grams of butter, 2 grams of sugar, small pinch of salt, 1 tablespoon of beaten egg, pinch of Vanilla extract and 5 grams of flour.


Chocolate Chip Cookie

All the ingredients of the plain cookie, plus 1 large tablespoon of chocolate chips.


Nut Chocolate Chip Cookie

All the ingredients of the Chocolate Chip cookie, and 1 tablespoon of mixed nuts.


The Plain Cookie contains all the basic ingredients for baking a no-nonsense no-frills cookie. The Chocolate Chip Cookie is the plain cookie, plus chocolate chips. The Nut Chocolate Chip cookie has all the ingredients of the Chocolate Chip Cookie, and a handful of mixed nuts. Classy!
